Rules and gameplay instructions

  1. Enter your name in the Chef Name slot and choose your level.
  2. A pizza with specific toppings will be displayed for 30 seconds.
  3. You must build the pizza using the ingredients and serve it before the time runs out by clicking on the Serve button.
  4. As you progress, the levels become harder as more ingredients are added to the pizzas, and the orders start to get more and more out of control. Keep your customers happy!
